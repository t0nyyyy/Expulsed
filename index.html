In a solar system carved up by untouchable conglomerates, Pepe’s just a young dreamer stuck with a name he didn’t ask for. Armed with nothing more than reckless hope, he taunts the cosmos—a lone flicker against the galaxy’s cold, titan glare. Young, naive, impulsive, a soul kissed by fortune one moment and kicked by it the next, Pepe is chasing a way out of Earth’s automated stagnation. His first stop: a grimy lunar outpost, where miners toil upon regolith claimed by capital-focused overlords who don’t give a damn. From lawless havens where outcasts shape their own rules to the relentless clutch of corporate power, Expulsed is a raw, grounded sci-fi book series that asks, what’s a nobody got to do to make it in a universe that doesn’t care? Thrust into a saga that cuts deep, blending heart, humour, and a fierce warning about the world we’re building—one misstep at a time. Explore the series.
